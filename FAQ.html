Registration on the website:

What is registration for?

  • Create own private account
  • Update or delete information in account
  • Get administration support
  • Buy and add goods to cart

How to register?

Go to page Sign up and fill the form, enter your email, login and password.

Ordering on the website:

How to place an order?

Go to page Home and choose the favourite product, it automatically will add to basket.

How to buy or delete the product?

After adding product to basket, you can enter end click button buy or delete current product.

How to payed?

After clicking buy in basket, you can pay for the product in the page Payment, enter your bank card and sum.

Product delivery via website:

How the goods are delivered?

Delivery across Ukraine is carried out by transport services "Nova Pochta", "Mist Express". In every city of Ukraine, you can take your order from our website.

How much does the delivery cost?

Delivery cost depends on the method of payment and type of delivery (courier or Nova Pochta / Mist Express).
